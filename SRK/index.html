Our Story

A Journey Through Ancient Spice Routes

Nestled in the heart of Cape Town, Spice Route Kitchen was born from a singular obsession — to honour the ancient trade paths that carried saffron from Kashmir, cinnamon from Ceylon, and cardamom from the Malabar Coast.

Our kitchen is a living archive. Every dish is a memory, every plate a destination. We believe that cooking is the most honest form of storytelling.
