Since 2002, Prestige Property Group has been at the forefront of South Africa's luxury real estate market. Founded on the principle that exceptional properties deserve exceptional representation, we have assembled a team of specialists who live and breathe luxury property.

PP Group is committed to resolving all complaints promptly, fairly and transparently. We take client feedback seriously and use it to continually improve our service.

News & Insights

Q4 2024: Luxury Market Update

The South African luxury residential market demonstrated continued resilience in Q4 2024, with the R10M+ segment recording a 12% increase in transaction volumes year-on-year. The Western Cape remains the most sought-after province, driven by semigration trends and foreign buyer interest.

Semigration to the Cape: A Sustained Trend

Internal migration from Johannesburg and Durban to the Western Cape and Garden Route continues to reshape the luxury market. PP Group's data shows that 38% of our Cape Town buyers in 2024 relocated from other provinces, prioritising lifestyle, safety and schooling.

Interest Rate Outlook for 2025

With the SARB expected to continue a cautious rate-cutting cycle in 2025, bond-financed buyers are returning to the luxury market with renewed confidence. PP Group advisors recommend buyers lock in current rates where possible.

International Buyer Activity

Enquiries from international buyers — particularly from the UK, Germany and the United States — have increased significantly, attracted by South Africa's relative value proposition and lifestyle offering. PP Group's international desk handled 47 cross-border transactions in 2024.
